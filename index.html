About Us

Welcome to Cupcake's Village, Hamilton’s first bakery devoted strictly to cupcakes. We offer a variety of gourmet cupcakes for all occasions. Birthdays, weddings, showers, gender reveals, anniversaries, corporate events, dinner parties or a simple afternoon treat…..cupcakes make any occasion more fun.

Cupcakes make a wonderful edible gift. We have many gift packages from our single greeting boxes (better than a greeting card) to our gift trays (a nice alternative to sending flowers).

At Cupcakes we are dedicated to helping you customize an order that will be perfect for you.

MENU

All of our cupcakes are baked in house daily using only the finest ingredients. We bake in small batches to ensure quality and freshness. Because of this we sometimes run out. We encourage you to place your order a least 24 hours in advance to ensure you get your favorites. Please come in or call us to place your order. If you would prefer use email, please allow 24 to 48 hours notice.
